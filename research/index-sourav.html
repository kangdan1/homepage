Current Project

  • Topic: Theory and computation of multicomponent, immisicble, multiphase flow in enhanced oil recovery from highly heterogeneous reservoirs.
    • Advisor: Dr. Prabir Daripa
    • Studying the different models (fractional flow, two-pressure) of Surfactant-Polymer flooding processes in EOR.
    • Developing a new and improved efficient, mass-conservative, multiscale and parallelizable fast solver that incorporates the complex chemical and physical processes involved. The model includes the effect of anisotropic dispersion and also the effect of capillarity.


    Workshop

    SAMSI Industrial Math/Stat Modeling Workshop
    Topic: Pajaro Valley Water Management                Advisors : Dr. E. W. Jenkins, Dr. M. W. Farthing
    • Designed a optimal basin infiltration network for managed aquifer recharge using real rainfall and topographical data of Pajaro Valley, California.
    • Used overland hydrologic model, CASC2D and TopoToolbox to compute design parameters needed for the multi-objective constrained optimization of the basin network.
