Current Project

  • Topic: Theory and computation of multicomponent, immiscible, multiphase flow in enhanced oil recovery from highly heterogeneous reservoirs.
    • Adviser: Dr. Prabir Daripa
    • Studying the different models (fractional flow, two-pressure) of Surfactant-Polymer flooding processes in EOR.
    • Developing a new and improved efficient, mass-conservative, multiscale and parallelizable fast solver that incorporates the complex chemical and physical processes involved. The model includes the effect of anisotropic dispersion and also the effect of capillarity.
