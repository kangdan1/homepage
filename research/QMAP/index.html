Quasiconformal Maps and Applications

Please visit this page as often as you please as new opportunities may arise for some of you interested in this area.

In solving many (NOT all) problems of classical physics, one essentially performs quasiconformal maps. Thus developing fast algorithms for generating quasiconformal maps is a fundamental problem. It is useful not only for solving applied problems but also for pure mathematics such as computing Teichmuller maps. To see this scope of Qmaps, Click here and here and here and here.
